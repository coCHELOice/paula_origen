# ORIGEN - Project Brief

## Project Overview
**Client / Brand:** Paula
**Project Name:** ORIGEN Landing Page & Strategy Prototype
**Goal:** Launch a high-conversion, premium landing page for the upcoming ORIGEN retreat. Also serves as a design prototype for the brand's broader digital direction.

## Target Audience
- High-trust individuals (likely female-identifying or women).
- High-achievers, professionals, or leaders who are holding immense responsibility.
- Experiencing burnout, dysregulation, or a profound need for an unstructured pause.
- Appreciates premium, aesthetic, and functional experiences.
- Skeptical of typical "woo-woo" wellness cliches and prefers a grounded, scientifically-informed, or highly pragmatic somatic approach.

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Clarity over cleverness.**
- **Warmth and femininity without vagueness.**
- **Premium, sober, trustworthy.**
- No bloated copy or empty wellness buzzwords.
- Direct, empathetic, and beautifully restrained.
