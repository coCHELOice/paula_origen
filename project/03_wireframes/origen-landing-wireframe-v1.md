# ORIGEN - Landing Page Wireframe V1

## 01. Hero Section
- **Visuals:** High-quality, moody but warm hero background. Perhaps a slow-motion video or a striking still showing nature or the premium venue.
- **Content:** Pre-headline, H1, Subheading, Primary CTA Button.
- **Layout:** Centered text or left-aligned with ample negative space.

## 02. The Catalyst
- **Visuals:** Calm, elegant typography section. No busy images.
- **Content:** H2 and 2 short paragraphs identifying the problem.
- **Layout:** Text-heavy but narrow max-width for readability.

## 03. The Invitation (Welcome)
- **Visuals:** High-quality image of women in a serene, luxurious environment holding space safely.
- **Content:** H2, 2 paragraphs introducing the retreat.
- **Layout:** Split layout (Image left / Text right).

## 04. The Experience (Pillars)
- **Visuals:** Minimalist icons or subtle structural dividers.
- **Content:** H2, 4 core pillars with titles and descriptions.
- **Layout:** 2x2 Grid or stacked cards.

## 05. The Location
- **Visuals:** Image gallery or carousel showing the premium rooms, nature, and food.
- **Content:** H2, 2 paragraphs.
- **Layout:** Text on top, large immersive gallery below. Full-width.

## 06. The Guide (Authority)
- **Visuals:** High-quality, unposed but professional portrait of Paula. Warm, approachable but authoritative.
- **Content:** H2, 2 paragraphs.
- **Layout:** Split layout (Text left / Image right).

## 07. Social Proof
- **Visuals:** Large, elegant typography. No headshots to prioritize privacy.
- **Content:** 2-3 testimonials.
- **Layout:** Carousel or subtle fade-in blocks.

## 08. Pricing & Logistics
- **Visuals:** Structured, clear list formatting.
- **Content:** Dates, location, investment, and inclusions.
- **Layout:** Minimalist pricing card or structured table-like list.

## 09. FAQ & Final Footer
- **Visuals:** Clean accordions.
- **Content:** FAQs, Final CTA, Footer links.
- **Layout:** Accordion interactions. Final CTA section to mirror the visual weight of the Hero section.
